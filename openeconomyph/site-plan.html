Overview

Purpose

One of the many driving factors of a progressive nation is its economy, this website advocates that the Philippines should open up its economy and remove unnecessary restrictions. This site will provide knowledge and understanding on explaining how opening the economy will better the lives of Filipinos. We also hope that the trend of Filipinos going abroad, to work in other countries due to the lack of opportunities here in their homeland will come to an end.

Audience

This site is created for the Filipino people anywhere in the world. Filipinos who want to have a progressive country. Filipinos who do not want the OFW trend to continue, to stop the brain drain of the Filipino people , where bright minds of Filipinos are utilitzed out of the country because of the lack of opportunities here. Filipinos that want better pay and better services. We call for an Open Economy Philippines

Content

Home page

OPENECONOMYPH.COM is created with the desire to improve the economic situation of the Philippines, to help improve the lives of many Filipinos, from private businesses by opening the economy to foreign investors and espousing competition to both local and foreign which is the drive of making better products and services as well as better salaries, and more work opportunities to Filipinos they will employ. The current situation of the Philippine economy needs more improvement and needs more competition. How the economy is run, affects our lives greatly and that through this website and the information provided may help educate many Filipinos and call for reforms in the economy. The site will tackle the “Why’s” of the needed economic reforms, as well as the disadvantages of it. A good economy affects the very personal lives of the people and should be taken seriously, that is why the site will also tackle the hard criticisms of letting foreigners into the Philippines Small, Medium or Large Enterprises. Concerns of foreign players taking over the economy should also be tackled and how the government create rules that will not make local or foreign investors at a disadvantage in using open market principles.

INFORMATION

In visiting the website people will have access to all the data that are required to have a basic understanding of the economy, as well as other related information that can help deepen their understanding of economic principles. Related articles and other information about how our protectionist constitutional law may or may not be helping our economic progress as a country as well as what other laws and policies the government has created towards a more open market economy. The site will also have a survey page that can also help gather data from its visitors regarding their economic understanding and their economic beliefs. The site might need a translated version to Tagalog , Cebuano, and other local languages to reach more Filipinos. A page will also be dedicated so that even grade schoolers will have also have a simple understanding of the economy and hopefully this will become one of the sites that will come up when searching about the Philippine economy. The Philippines although is not a closed economy but how the economy works seems more protectionist than capitalist.

Free Market Road Map

A page will also be dedicated on how the Philippines will become a true open market. This page will also include the Roadblocks that the Philippines as a nation should overcome. May it be from the government , private business , communities or individuals this website will find their questions and the possible answers that are available online or by anyone who is willing to share their knowledge or if needed even to get the services of economists that can share their knowledge

A page will also be dedicated to Correct Movement. As the creator being able to gain understanding and knowledge of how the Philippines could achieve it potentials a page dedicated to the Correct Movement will also be dedicated. Here the other 2 agendas will be shared namely 1. Parliamentary System and 2. Federalism. The movement pushes for 3PA or the 3 point agenda and for the creator of this site, creating a better economic atmosphere or Opening the Economy to Foreign Direct Investors and Business is the priority to fuel the other 2 agendas as already mentioned. One of the co-founders of this movement is Mr. Orion Dumdum who himself is an OFW working in Singapore and have researched and studied all the needed facts that are needed to a better Philippines who is also a creator of the website correctphilippines.org that has the complete of the 3 point agenda that the movement is advocating. Realizing that one person – the President – can never fix one problem by himself , this sub page will be given more to the parliamentary system information on how more heads are better given the opportunity to govern and make important decisions for the future of our country.
